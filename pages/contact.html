Quick answers

Frequently asked questions

Where can I find my reservation ID?+

Open My Bookings and choose Manage on the relevant reservation. The ID appears in the reservation details.

Can I change or cancel a reservation?+

Use My Bookings for available self-service options, or choose Reservation Help or Cancellation in this form.

How do I report a table selection problem?+

Choose Table Selection and include the restaurant, date, table, and reservation ID when available.

Is this request sent by email?+

No. This project records support requests in this browser. You can use the direct email link for an email conversation.
